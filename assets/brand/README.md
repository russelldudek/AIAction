# Employer identity asset decision

Covington & Burling LLP is identified through a restrained plain-text nominative reference.

The campaign does not include or recreate the firm's logo, proprietary photography, website assets, or font files. The public source record documents a specific Terms of Use restriction on displaying or using Covington marks without prior permission.

The candidate-created visual system is therefore original: midnight navy, paper, cobalt, and brass; editorial typography; fine rules; and the Living Precedent Engine.
