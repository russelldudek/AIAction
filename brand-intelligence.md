# Brand Intelligence

- Public identity: Covington presents itself as sophisticated, collaborative, commercially practical, and strong at the intersection of litigation, investigations, corporate work, policy, and regulation.
- Public design direction: Living Group describes the refreshed identity as smart, formal, thoughtful, measured, intelligent, and grounded in collaboration and foresight.
- Trademark restriction: Covington's Terms of Use state that its marks may not be displayed or used without prior permission.
- Implementation decision: no logo or copied company asset. Plain-text nominative company name only. Candidate-authored navy, paper, blue, and brass palette directionally recognizes the public identity but is not represented as an official color specification.
- Typography: Georgia and Arial/system substitutes. No proprietary font files copied.
- Original candidate metaphor: Applied AI Reporter / workflow precedent.
