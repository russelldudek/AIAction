A 120-day entry path

Begin by reading how the firm works, then make one portfolio mechanism useful, prove a bounded set of workflows, and only then expand the precedent system.

Days 0-30

Read the practice

  • Shadow attorneys and matter teams.
  • Map demand, knowledge, controls, vendors, and ownership.
  • Establish team cadence and delivery expectations.
Days 31-60

Concentrate the portfolio

  • Define intake and sequencing criteria.
  • Select bounded high-value workflows.
  • Agree evaluation and authority before build.
Days 61-90

Prove and enable

  • Ship with named review and support.
  • Run demos, office hours, and feedback loops.
  • Measure adoption, quality, and operating value.
Days 91-120

Publish precedent

  • Promote reusable patterns with exceptions.
  • Clarify maintenance and retirement ownership.
  • Report the next portfolio decisions to leadership.
