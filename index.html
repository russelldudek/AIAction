Read the mandate

Mandate behind the title

The firm is building an operating layer between AI strategy and daily legal work.

The director’s job is to concentrate demand, equip practice-aligned managers, advance AI and knowledge in lockstep, and turn proven delivery into maintained firm capability.
01

Read practice demand

Start with matter execution, attorney judgment, client friction, and the real objective beneath the first request.

02

Build a defensible pattern

Bind workflow design to approved knowledge, retained authority, task-specific evaluation, controls, adoption, and value evidence.

03

Publish maintained precedent

Scale only what survives practice variation. Preserve exceptions, name owners, observe performance, and retire stale patterns.

Cost of leaving the tension unresolved

Pilots multiply, knowledge and controls lag, lawyers rebuild local workarounds, and the firm loses the evidence needed to distinguish scalable capability from one-off success.

The contradiction field

The work lives in the tensions a model cannot make disappear.

Partner trust comes from making the tradeoff visible, preserving the right authority, and showing what evidence earns the next scope decision.
01
Practice judgmentandfirm-wide reuse

Standardize controls and reusable components while preserving legitimate matter and practice exceptions.

02
Speed and leverageandprivilege and defensibility

Scale authority, review, evidence, access, and escalation with consequence and sensitivity.

03
Model capabilityandknowledge readiness

Advance workflows and their source content, taxonomies, ownership, and freshness in lockstep.

04
Pilot enthusiasmandportfolio concentration

Focus the team on use cases with a clear client-service outcome, accountable owner, adoption path, and measurable evidence.
