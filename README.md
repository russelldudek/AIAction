# Russell Dudek - Director of Applied AI Candidate Vision

An independent candidate campaign for the Covington & Burling LLP Director of Applied AI opportunity.

The campaign argues that the role should build a growing body of trusted workflow precedent: practice-specific AI-enabled work that earns broader reuse through explicit knowledge, retained human authority, task-specific evaluation, adoption ownership, value evidence, exceptions, maintenance, and retirement criteria.

## Living Precedent Engine

The candidate vision uses a seven-layer interactive record:

1. Fact Pattern
2. Knowledge Record
3. Human Authority
4. Evaluation
5. Adoption and Ownership
6. Value Evidence
7. Precedent Treatment

Visitors can compare Corporate, Litigation, Regulatory, and White Collar workflows and resolve each to a matter prototype, practice pattern, cross-practice candidate, or maintained firm standard.
